About Broadcast Planner

Broadcast Planner is a web-based scheduling interface designed to help viewers and media planners track TV and radio programming in a clean and organized way. Whether you're tuning in for the morning news or planning your team's radio lineup, this tool helps you keep up-to-date with programs across various networks.

Who is it for?

  • Media professionals and broadcasters
  • TV and radio audiences
  • Event planners and journalists

Features

  • Daily program schedules
  • Toggle between TV and Radio views
  • Search by program or channel
